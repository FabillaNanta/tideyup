about us!

Tideyup is a web-based app that is able to help researchers to maintain a structured observation data and notes that is exclusively used for waves observation. You can fill out the form and put everything you need to put there. Then, in the review session feature, you can recap all of your data that you previously inputed on the tidepad feature.

fabilla & ita marine information system


how it works!
  • Go to the tidepad feature on your user homepage
  • This feature already provide the common data that you usually get on your observation, so simply fill out the form and you can add your personal notes as well!
  • Then go ahead and move to our review session feature. Tadaah! your data that you previously inserted are going to show up there!
  • Gently reminder! On the data ID section, you can freely choose your own ID! If the data doesn't appear on the review session feature, please choose a different ID!
  • Have fun and good luck!
